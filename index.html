Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this event for?

+

Anyone who wants to take control of their health — from wellness-curious beginners to experienced biohackers, health professionals, entrepreneurs, and executives. If you follow the longevity and biohacking movement — Blueprint, Huberman Lab, Dr. Mark Hyman, Bulletproof, the anti-aging science community — and want that caliber of knowledge in one accessible, affordable day — this is for you.

Who is this event NOT for?

+

This isn't a medical consultation or a substitute for working with your doctor. It's also not a passive lecture series — it's an immersive, high-energy day for people who are ready to take action on their health.

What's the difference between General Admission and VIP?

+

Both include all keynotes, meals, expo access, and networking. VIP adds metabolic testing, Phase Contrast Microscopy blood testing, a microbiome test assessment kit at a discount, priority seating, and a speaker meet-and-greet.

What if I buy a ticket and can't make it?

+

All tickets are fully refundable up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets are transferable to another person.

Is parking available?

+

Parking will be free on-site or discounted (max $10) at 1–3 nearby public lots within a 2–3 block walk. Parking details and validation instructions will be sent by email a few weeks before the event. ♿ ADA-accessible parking options are available at or near the venue. Contact us for additional info.

What about food and dietary restrictions?

+

Breakfast, lunch, and snacks are included with every ticket. We accommodate common dietary needs — please note any restrictions when you register.

How is this different from watching podcasts and reading books?

+

Podcasts and books give you information. This event gives you a curated, distilled plan — plus face-to-face access to experts you can actually ask YOUR specific questions to, and a room full of 120+ people on the same journey. The networking alone is worth the ticket.

I've never been to a health event before. Will I feel out of place?

+

Not at all. Our attendees range from total beginners to seasoned biohackers. The speakers are chosen specifically because they can make complex science accessible. You'll feel welcome from the moment you walk in.

Are scholarship tickets available?

+

Yes — we offer scholarship tickets for individuals actively working to reverse obesity or metabolic syndrome. Contact us for details.

What should I wear?

+

Conference dress is casual, as our exhibiting partners will be hosting demonstrations and you want to be comfortable. Due to the stage lighting required for filming (and to keep our presenters from melting), the Main Stage room will be kept VERY COOL, so we highly recommend layered clothing, including a hoodie, wrap, or jacket.
